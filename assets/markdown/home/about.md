## Introduction

Hi. Welcome to my site.

My name is Kevin Huang. I am currently working on a PhD in Electrical Engineering at University of Southern California as a part of the [SAIL](https://sail.usc.edu/) lab under Shrikanth Narayanan. I've obtained a Masters (2021) and a Bachelors (2020) in Computer Science at UCSD. 

My current research is in studying speech production at the articulatory level using MRI data. I have a particular interest in **Audio Processing** (analysis and synthesis) in the domains of music and speech, studying methods from both traditional signal processing and state of the art machine learning. I also have some interest in Computer Graphics on the side (particularly real-time graphics, animation, and virtual reality).

At USC, I am currently working with [SAIL](https://sail.usc.edu) (Signal Analysis and Interpretation Laboratory) under Prof. Shrikanth Narayanan. My research primarily falls under studying speech production with the interdisciplinary [SPAN](https://sail.usc.edu/span/) group, but I also am involved in other projects involving developing speech analysis pipelines.

Asides from programming, I enjoy playing piano and playing games. Although I am classically trained, taking piano lessons since elementary school, I more often than not tend to play anime song covers on piano. As for games, I mainly play gacha games, rhythm games, minesweeper, or whatever my friends are playing at the time.
